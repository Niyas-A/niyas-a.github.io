Niyas Attasseri

I am a masters student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of California San Diego. I'm specializing in Intelligent Systems, Robotics and Control as part of the Contextual Robotics Institute (CRI).

I have a B.Tech in Chemical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Madras, where I worked at SENAI lab and Centre For Innovation IIT Madras in the Mars Rover project.
