Niyas Attasseri

I am a masters student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of California San Diego. I'm specializing in Intelligent Systems, Robotics and Control as part of the Contextual Robotics Institute (CRI). I have completed all my program requirements and am open to full-time and co-op opportunities starting in January 2025.

I earned my bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering (Systems and Control track) from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, where I worked at SENAI lab and Centre For Innovation IIT Madras on the Mars Rover project. Over the years at IIT Madras and later during my research on building autonomous bicycle, I have developed a deep interest towards Robotics, Controls, Vision, Machine Learning and related areas.

My interests

I am passionate about a broad range of topics that span both theoretical and applied areas:

1. Broad Theoretical Areas: Robotics, Control Theory, Systems Theory, Computer Vision, Optimization, Machine Learning, Algorithms

2. Specific Focus Areas: Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM), State Estimation, Sensor Fusion, Adaptive Control, Motion Planning, Perception, Planning Algorithms, Reinforcement Learning and Deep Learning for Vision.

3. Application Areas: Mobile Robots (Ground, Aerial, Underwater), Multi-robot and Networked Systems, Autonomous Systems, Driverless Cars, Field and Service Robots, Guidance and Navigation for Autonomous Systems

I am actively seeking opportunities in Robotics, Computer Vision, Automatic Control, Machine Learning, and related fields. I am open to Engineering, R&D, or Research roles in these areas.
